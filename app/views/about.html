About me

My name is Andres O. Serrano and I am from Santa Fe, New Mexico. After several years of general studies and numerous "Hello World" tutorials, I decided to begin my career in working with the exiting world wide web as Quality Assurance tester and later as a Front-End Developer. Perhaps it was the order of positions that has better shaped my skillset and influenced my "OCD" to pay closer attention to detail.

My work ranges from web design and prototyping to mobile and web app development. Over the years I aquired a genuine interest in a variety of programming languages and frameworks which has given me the advantage of quickly adapting to new or existing projects and overall flexibility.
